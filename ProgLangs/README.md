# Syllabus

### Every week

- A quiz during the lectures.
- A quiz in the beginning of the practice classes.

### Exam period

- Assignment.
- Final exam - theory (20 points) + practice (50 points).

## Grade calculation

The final score is produced simply by adding the score components described above. No component has a minimum requirement individually. Based on your score out of a total of 100, your grade is:

- 80 or more points → 5
- 70 or more points → 4
- 60 or more points → 3
- 50 or more points → 2
- Under 50 points → 1
